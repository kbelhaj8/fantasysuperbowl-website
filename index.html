About The League

The Fantasy Superbowl league was first established in 2006 on Yahoo! Sports platform by avid football fans from the Binghmaton, NY area. This is a keeper league with a deep bench and very active players. The first drafts were all in-person at various local landmarks. While some of the rules might slightly change from year to year, at its core, this is a keeper league where managers have to keep up to 6 veterans and 1 rookie each year.
